# Caleb Chess

A browser-based chess experience featuring full rule enforcement, two-player hot seat play, and a configurable computer opponent built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## Getting Started

1. Clone the repository.
2. Open `index.html` in any modern browser.
3. Use the main menu to pick a battle mode (single-player or two-player), adjust the AI difficulty when available, toggle sound, and review the leaderboard. Press **Play** to enter the board.

## Features

- **Legal move enforcement** – every piece adheres to chess rules, including castling, en passant, and promotion.
- **Check and checkmate detection** – the game automatically announces check, checkmate, or stalemate.
- **Two-player support** – pass-and-play with alternating turns (white moves first).
- **Computer opponent** – battle an AI powered by a minimax search with four selectable depths: Easy, Normal, Hard, and Pro.
- **Polished presentation** – premium wood-and-metal board styling, hand-crafted 3D piece sculpts (including a crowned queen and horse-shaped knight), and smooth movement animation keep the action cinematic.
- **Immersive feedback** – unique sound cues accompany moves, captures, and check events.
- **Guided entry** – a full-screen main menu lets you configure mode, difficulty, sound, and review a sample leaderboard before starting play.
- **Responsive layout** – controls and the board adapt fluidly from desktops to phones and tablets.
- **Accessible controls** – keyboard and mouse friendly square selection with visual move hints.

Press **Restart** at any time to reset with the current mode and difficulty. Use the in-game sound toggle to mute or re-enable effects without leaving the board. When playing against the computer you can choose to control either white or black pieces from the menu.
